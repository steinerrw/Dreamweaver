About Us


"Almost a Song" is a traditional old-time Appalachian string band which has evolved from its root as a jam band to one which performs in the Fairmont, WV area over the last two years. We bring live music to small communities to help in their celebrations or events.
We are always learning new songs as there is so much music that still needs to be discovered so we can pass it down to future musicians. The band is always open to new performers in the area with the same goals and desires and have even created micro groups within ourselves which ultimately make the entire group better.
We hope you enjoyed some of the music and if you get a chance to see us live, have the opportunity to stop by and listen or play, your always welcome.

Biography




"Appalachain String Project - A.S.P." is a group of musicians who are working on preserving the traditional styles of Appalachian "Old-time" and Irish music with a core group and other members who are constantly changing...but focused on the 'style'.
